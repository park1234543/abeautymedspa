FAQ

  • How do I book a spa appointment?
    Open the app, browse services, select a time slot, and confirm your booking.

  • How do I cancel or reschedule?
    Go to "My Bookings" in the app and tap the booking you want to change.

  • I forgot my password. What do I do?
    Use the "Forgot Password" option on the login screen to reset via email.

  • Is my personal data safe?
    Yes. We encrypt all personal information and never share it with third parties.

  • How do I delete my account?
    Go to Profile > Settings > Delete Account, or email us to request deletion.

Privacy
