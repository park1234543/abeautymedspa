## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I make a booking?**  
A: Open the app, browse our services, select a time slot, and confirm your booking.

**Q: How do I cancel or reschedule?**  
A: Go to "My Bookings" in the app and tap the booking you want to modify.

**Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?**  
A: On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and enter your email address.

**Q: Can I use the app without creating an account?**  
A: Yes — tap "Browse without signing in" on the welcome screen to explore the app as a guest.

**Q: Which payment methods are accepted?**  
A: Payments are processed securely in-app. Contact us for details.
